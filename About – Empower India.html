Our Mission and Commitment to Sustainability

As a prominent sustainable energy solutions provider in India, we specialize in solar power generation, wind power generation, green power, biofuel production, and various recycling initiatives. We believe in creating value not only for our stakeholders but also for the environment and society as a whole. By operating top-performing renewable energy assets, we ensure that our efforts benefit everyone involved.

Our vision towards shaping green future

In Indian culture, wind, water, earth, fire, and sky have been highly honored due to spiritual beliefs. At Empower, we are dedicated to reducing our reliance on fossil fuels and promoting renewable energy as the cornerstone of the future energy landscape. 

We’re focused on using India’s plentiful solar and wind energy to fuel sustainable development, recognizing renewable energy as key for the future.

At Empower India Limited, we see renewable energy as not just a business opportunity but a means to empower communities and foster economic development. With a diverse interest and knowledge of green energy, we are committed to pursuing organic and inorganic growth avenues, including collaborations, acquisitions, and strategic investments.

Our objectives

Our goal is to become a trusted sustainability partner for donor organizations such as corporations, governments, social entrepreneurs, and non-governmental organizations (NGOs).
